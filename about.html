About Shelly

Hello, I'm Shelly

I’m not a care agency — I’m simply someone with a deep love for the elderly and a passion for their wellbeing. Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of working closely with older adults, learning just how much joy, connection, and gentle support can mean in the later years of life.

I completed my Honours Degree in Psychology through UNISA, where my thesis explored enhancing the quality of life in the ageing process. Today, I’m a registered Specialist Wellness Counsellor with ASCHP, focusing on elder mental wellness — offering a listening ear, guidance, and heartfelt companionship.

Alongside emotional support, I offer gentle movement and exercise sessions, often followed by a cup of tea and a chat — because I believe friendships, laughter, and belonging are just as important as physical health.

All my services are personal — nothing is outsourced. I’m the one who walks the journey with you or your loved one, ensuring continuity, understanding, and trust.

Shelly’s Circle of Care is flexible and shaped around each person’s unique needs. Whether it’s advice, emotional support, coordination, or simply bringing more meaning into everyday moments — whatever your needs, I’ll always help with genuine care and compassion from the heart.

That's where I come in — think of me as an "almost-a-Family on call."
